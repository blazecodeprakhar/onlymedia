FAQs

What does OnlyMedia do?

OnlyMedia is a strategic media solutions partner focused on creating impactful media strategies that drive measurable growth. We go beyond ad placements, We align every campaign with your business objectives, using data, custom planning, and continuous optimization across platforms like Meta, Google, YouTube, DV360, OTT, and Transit Media.

Why do businesses need a custom media plan?

Because one-size-fits-all media strategies don’t work. A custom media plan ensures:

  • Your audience is reached with precision
  • Budgets are optimized for ROI
  • Messaging aligns with your business goals

At OnlyMedia, we build bespoke media plans that are dynamic, data-driven, and performance-focused.

What is a data-driven media strategy and why is it important?

A data-driven strategy uses real-time analytics, audience behavior insights, and predictive modeling to remove guesswork from media buying. It helps you:

  • Identify high-intent audiences
  • Optimize ad spend
  • Measure what truly matters—business growth

Read: [The Ultimate Guide to Developing a Data-Driven Media Strategy].

How does OnlyMedia measure success?

We measure success using SMART KPIs—Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound. Metrics we track include:

  • Awareness: Reach, impressions
  • Engagement: CTR, interactions
  • Conversions: CPA, ROAS, leads
    We also provide real-time dashboards and detailed reports so you know where every rupee is going.
